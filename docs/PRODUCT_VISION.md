# CreatorKit AI Product Vision

CreatorKit AI is a draft-quality scoring tool for creators. Users paste a content idea, caption, post draft, or transcript and receive:

- a performance score
- a short critique
- practical suggestions before publishing

The product is meant to help creators move faster with more confidence. It should answer three questions quickly:

1. Will this perform well?
2. What is weak or unclear?
3. What should I change before I publish?

## Primary Users

- solo creators publishing on short-form and social platforms
- content marketers reviewing drafts before launch
- creators testing hooks, captions, and calls to action

## Core Value

- reduce guesswork before posting
- turn vague creative instincts into actionable feedback
- surface weaknesses early, when edits are cheap

## Long-Term Direction

CreatorKit AI can grow from a heuristic scorer into a platform with saved analyses, user history, content libraries, team workflows, and model-backed recommendations.
